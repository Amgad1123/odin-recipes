Pomegranate Fizz

Description

Refreshing fizzy pomegranate beverage, the perfect pairing for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon agve syrup or honey
  • 1 fluid ounce fresh lime juice
  • 3 fluid ounces pomegranate juice
  • 1 tablespoon piloncillo, or to taste
  • 4 fluid ounces club soda
  • ice cubes
  • 1 lime wedge

Steps

  1. In a shaker, add basil leaves with lime juice and agave syrup
  2. Mash with a cocktail muddler
  3. Add pomegranate juice to the shaker and shake well
  4. Fill glass with ice cubes and strain contents into the glass
  5. Top with club soda and garnish with lime wedge
