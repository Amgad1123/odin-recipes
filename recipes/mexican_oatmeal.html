Mexican Oatmeal

Description

Heartwarming delicious oatmeal perfect for a meal at anytime of the day!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 Mexican cinnammon stick
  • 1/2 cup quick-cookng oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon piloncillo, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring water and cinnamon stick to boil in a saucepan
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Add oats and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add milk and cook until thickened, 1 or 2 minutes
  5. Remove from heat and stir in piloncillo, cinnamon, and vanilla
